Analyze and debug your circuits with AI

Fuse is a service that is dedicated to helping everyone with their hardware projects. Simply upload your circuit to our circuit analyzer, and Fuse will intelligently analyze and provide feedback for your circuit.

Current Components Supported

Our AI can currently identify with high levels of certainty resistors that are present on a breadboard. We plan to train our AI on datasets of other components in the future, such as LEDs and capacitors.
